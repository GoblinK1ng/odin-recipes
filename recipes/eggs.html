Hamburgers

Description

Fluffy scrambled eggs: a simple delight. Whisk eggs with milk, cook in butter until softly set, and season to taste. Perfect for a comforting breakfast.

Ingriedents

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs with milk.
  2. Cook in butter.
  3. Season.
  4. Enjoy!
